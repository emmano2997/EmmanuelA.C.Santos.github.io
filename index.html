Perfil Profissional


BALANCED
people with this profile focus both on their careers and on the search for reconciling their personal and professional lives. They may be highly motivated by work, but understand that it must integrate into their life as a whole. These are people who want flexibility above all else and always assess the impact of their career on their family and lifestyle.


SPECIALIST
people with this profile are full of energy and initiative, they prefer a career of specialization through courses and practices that stimulate their aptitudes and interests. They are motivated when they are “experts” in a particular subject and seek challenging jobs that test their knowledge and skills in the area. They usually aim for positions that allow them to continue in their specialty in a certain area (technical managers).


STABLE
people with this profile need to feel safe in the work environment. They seek greater predictability of the future. In other words, they want to “know where they are stepping”. They are looking for companies and sectors that convey an image of solidity and reliability, providing stability and good benefit programs to their employees. It is stability, mainly financial, that will guide the careers of these professionals.
